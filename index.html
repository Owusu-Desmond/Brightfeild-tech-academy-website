About

Brightfield Tech Academy is a social venture that seeks to offer Tech training and hub for residents of Sefwi grooming them to become tech-ready for jobs in the software industry. Brightfield Tech Academy aim is to help people to Connect with other techies to learn the latest technologies and build software products. Brightfield Tech Academy offer software programs such as Web App Development and Mobile App Development.In BrightfieldTech Academy we have Early Staters cohorts, Explorers Pack and Kids for Coding.
